The Challenge

Drones operating in complex environments - whether in defense, security, or search & rescue - are limited by conventional camera systems. These systems struggle with dynamic lighting conditions, generate massive amounts of redundant data, and require high bandwidth, which is often unavailable in the field.

Our Solution

OccipitAI leverages event-based cameras to capture only changes in the frame, drastically reducing data load while improving performance in high dynamic range (HDR) conditions. We use generative AI to synthetically create training datasets and reconstruct usable images from event data, bypassing the need for costly, hard-to-collect real-world data.
